Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start receiving on Sendcash?

You’ll need to create an account to get started. You’ll also need to provide a valid government-issued ID and a utility bill that’s less than three months old if you plan to create a virtual account.

Can I receive payments to my USD Domiciliary account?

No, you can only receive transfers in your Naira bank account.

How much does it cost to create virtual accounts & payment links?

They are free of charge.

How much are your transaction fees?

Transfers to your virtual account cost you 1% of the amount received, while fees on payment links are incurred by your sender & vary from little to nothing based on the payment option they choose.

Can I send ACH transfers to my USD virtual account?

No, we only accept wire transfers at the moment.

Can I send other currencies to my virtual account?

No, you can only send the stated currency.
